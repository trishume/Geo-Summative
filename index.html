Falling For Niagara

July 2011


Choose Your Version:

Fully Fancy
The Fully fancy version includes a simulation of the drive to Niagara.
It requires Google Chrome and the Google Earth plugin. If it doesn't load the first time, reload.

Normal
The normal version uses Google maps instead of Google Earth.
It works properly in Firefox or Chrome. The map doesn't work properly in Internet Explorer.

Low Tech
Low tech version with no map. Some formatting problems
Good for just reading the write ups.
